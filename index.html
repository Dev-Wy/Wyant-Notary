What is a Mobile Notary?

A mobile notary travels to their client. Rather than you venturing out in bad weather, in the midst of a workday, or during off hours when most notaries are closed. I would love to offer you convenient, personalized notarization at your home or business.

What is Our Specialty?

We specialize in assisting those whose time and energy is valuable. We want everyone in all communities to be safe and empowered in their unique situations.

Who Benefits from Our Mobile Notary Service?

  • - Businesses desiring weekend or evening notarization
  • - Rehabilitation and nursing homes and their residents
  • - Hospitals patients
  • - Homebound individuals
  • - Stay-at-Home parents
  • - Members of POC and the LGBTQIA+ communities
  • - Disabled veterans

Disclaimers

I do not provide vehicle notary services such as title and tag exchanges at this time.

I am not an attorney licensed to practice law in this Commonwealth. I am not allowed to draft legal records, give advice on legal matters, including immigration, or charge a fee for those activities.

A Pennsylvania Notary is not permitted to charge more than the published rates on the PA Department of State website for a notarial stamp. If you encounter a PA Notary Public charging more than the below rates, please contact the Pennsylvania Association of Notaries (PAN) at 1-800-944-8790.

Many Notaries do however assess administrative fees to cover their travel or their overhead such as rent, copies, and record keeping. These fees are appropriate as long as they are indicated as separate from the State fee, and they may vary from notary to notary.
